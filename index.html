About MedVerify

Learn about our mission to combat counterfeit medicines using blockchain technology

The Problem

Counterfeit medicines are a global health crisis, causing an estimated 1 million deaths annually. These fake drugs often contain incorrect ingredients, improper dosages, or harmful substances, putting patients at risk and undermining trust in healthcare systems.

Our Solution

MedVerify leverages blockchain technology to create an immutable record of medicine authenticity. By minting each medicine batch as a non-fungible token (NFT) on the Archway blockchain, we establish a transparent and tamper-proof verification system.

How Blockchain Solves It

  • Immutable Records: Once recorded, medicine data cannot be altered or deleted
  • Transparency: All stakeholders can verify authenticity through the blockchain
  • Security: Cryptographic verification prevents counterfeiting
  • Traceability: Complete history of each medicine batch from manufacturer to patient
